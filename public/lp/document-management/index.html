Centralized Document Repository

Store all loan-related documents: applications, bank statements, tax returns, term sheets, executed agreements, and correspondence. Auto-organize by borrower, loan, and document type. Search by filename, content, or metadata.

Version Control & Document History

Track document versions with upload timestamp, user, and change notes. Retrieve prior versions for audits or disputes. Prevent document overwrites with automatic versioning.

Role-Based Access & Permissions

Control who can view, download, upload, or delete documents. Restrict access to sensitive documents (tax returns, bank statements) to underwriters and admins. Audit every document access with user and timestamp.

OCR & Data Extraction

Extract data from bank statements, tax forms, and invoices with OCR. Pre-populate application fields from uploaded documents. Reduce manual data entry and transcription errors.

Security & Compliance

Encrypt documents at rest and in transit (AES-256, TLS 1.3). Maintain document retention policies per regulatory requirements. Export loan files for audits, portfolio sales, or legal proceedings.
