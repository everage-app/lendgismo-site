ACH & Card Payment Processing

Charge borrowers via ACH debit, debit cards, or credit cards using Stripe. Support one-time payments, recurring schedules, and custom payment plans. Handle partial payments, prepayments, and manual adjustments.

Automated Payment Retries

Configure retry logic for failed ACH transactions and declined cards. Automatically attempt payments 1-3 times with smart spacing. Send payment failure notifications via SMS and email.

Stripe webhooks auto-update payment status in real-time. Reconcile deposits to loan accounts, fees, and interest. Export payment histories for accounting systems and investor reports.

Refunds, Disputes & Chargebacks

Handle refunds and dispute management through integrated Stripe dashboard. Track chargeback activity and link to borrower accounts. Automated alerts for dispute escalations.
