Collateral Management & Borrowing Base

Track eligible collateral categories (A/R, inventory, equipment) with automated advance rate calculations. Generate borrowing base certificates, monitor loan-to-value ratios, and set alerts for covenant breaches. Support for AR aging, inventory turns, and appraisal tracking.

Field Exam & Audit Workflows

Document field exams with photo uploads, collateral verification checklists, and inspector notes. Link findings to borrower profiles and flag discrepancies for follow-up. Export audit trails for compliance and investor reporting.

Underwriting for ABL Use Cases

Configure credit policies specific to asset-based lending: advance rates by collateral type, concentration limits, cross-aging schedules, and dilution analysis. Automate eligibility checks and generate exception reports for manual review.

Integrations for ABL Lenders

Connect QuickBooks and accounting systems for real-time AR/AP data. Integrate with Plaid for operating account monitoring and cash sweeps. Use Stripe for automated ACH payments and fee collections.
